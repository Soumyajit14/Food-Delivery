Help & Support

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our food categories like Pizza, Sushi, or Burgers. On the item page, use the "+" button to add items to your cart. When you're ready, click on "Cart" in the navigation bar to proceed to checkout.

How does online payment work?

When you choose "Pay Via Online" at checkout, you will be redirected to a payment page. This page will show a QR code with the total amount. Simply scan this QR code with any UPI app (like Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.) to complete your payment.

How do I book a table at a restaurant?

Navigate to the "Restaurants" section and select a restaurant. On the restaurant's page, you will find a booking form. Select your desired date, time, and number of guests to see the estimated price. Follow the steps to confirm and pay for your booking.

Can I apply a coupon to my order?

Yes! On the cart/checkout page, there is a "Coupon Code" field. Enter your coupon code and click "Apply". If the code is valid, the discount will be reflected in your order summary.
